About this Course

This is my study note of the free course named Design of Computer Programs from Udacity.

This course teaches how to approach programming problems and how to fight against the complexity of programs, which are the most important skills for a programmer to possess.

The professor, Peter Norvig, teaches these skills through a pose-try-feedback loop.

The lessons consists of small programming problems. He makes us solve them first, and then shows his own solution as feedback. Most of the time, I was surprised by his elegant solutions.

This is one of my favorite computer courses of all time.

Lessons

  • Lesson 1: Poker
  • Lesson 2: Zebra Puzzle, Cryptarithmetic
  • Lesson 3: Regex, Parser
  • Lesson 4: Water Pouring, Bridge, Missionaries and Cannibals
  • Lesson 5: Porcine Probability
  • Lesson 6: Word Games
  • Lesson 7: Practice Exam
